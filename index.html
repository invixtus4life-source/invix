Mandatory Compliance Disclosure

Fiduciary Risk Protocol

Transparency and structural principal preservation are the foundations of Zennith. Please review our strict liquidity rules and capital protection guarantees.

The 60/40 Preservation Guarantee

To structurally mitigate catastrophic market exposure, Zennith enforces a quantitative operational boundary: our trading algorithms are restricted to a maximum market exposure of 40% of aggregate deposited capital.

60% Cold Reserve Shield

The remaining 60% of all capital is held in isolated, non-traded institutional reserves. Should the firm ever face structural dissolution, this 60% is contractually ring-fenced to be returned immediately to our active investors.

Terms of Liquidity

  • 90-Day Initial Maturation

    To ensure statistical reliability for our trading volume, newly deposited principal is subject to a mandatory 90-day stabilization lock-in period.

  • 45-Day Profit Distributions

    Following activation, your generated yield becomes fully available for withdrawal to your designated accounts at the end of every 45-day cycle.

  • Daily Principal Access (Post-90 Days)

    Upon completion of the 90-day lock-in, your primary capital becomes fully liquid, permitting daily withdrawal requests at your discretion.
